Classroom Schedule/Expectations

The students of Fun Happy Cool Pre-School will take short but well-managed school-time from Monday to Friday.

Your students' class will start at 8:30 and finish at 12:30. Campus itself opens at 8: 00, and will close at 1: 00, so be sure your kid(s) will be dropped off and picked up by those respective times.

Schedule

  • 8:00 - 8:30 Gate opens
  • 8:30 - 8:45 Morning Tubs and Organization
  • 8:45 - 9:00 Schedule Overview and Circle Time
  • 9:00 - 9:45 Centers and Small Group Activities
  • 9:45 - 10:00 Reading activities
  • 10:00 - 10:30 Outdoors time (playground/recess)
  • 10:30 - 11:10 Math activities and more Small Group Activities
  • 11:10 - 11:40 Lunch Time
  • 11:50 - 12:30 Interactive Activities and Social Time
  • 12:30 - 1:00 Parent Pickup

Expectations/Safety

The classroom will be managed with utmost care from the adults on campus. The teachers, administrators, and nurses will tend to the students' needs. It is also expected that the behaviour of the children in class abide by those expected from the teachers and the campus as a whole. If found necessary, the adults on campus may call the parents of the student in order to address any visible issues.

If any emergencies are present among the students, there will be eminent notification to the entire campus and the parents in order to create situational awareness. As for the parents in need for their kids on campus, the adults on campus are responsible for responding accordingly and appeasing to the needs of the parents in case of any emergency or eminent need of picking up the student.

The condition of the classroom, lunchroom and playground are maintained at utmost responsibility for the sake of childrens' safety and cleanliness. The adults on campus will be sure to regularly clean the classroom environment, the lunchroom, bathrooms and the playground. Here in Fun Happy Cool Pre-School, safety and cleanliness is one of our top priorities.

Fun Happy Cool Pre-School® provides equal services regardless of the individual's race, religion, or gender. Those in need will be supported accordingly, and our campus strives to give every student equal opportunity.
